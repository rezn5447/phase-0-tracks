This is a webpage designed to help you understand cats (they are very complex!)

Here is a list of info on cats
  • They are adorable
  • They make cute sounds
  • They have sharp claws!
  • They aren't dangerous unless provoked
If you happen to stumble across a cat in the wild and are unsure of what to do, follow these steps:
  1. Stop and make sure the cat has noticed you
  2. Mimic meowing sounds so the cat is tricked into thinking you are also a cat
  3. Pretend to have fur on your hands and start licking your hands with your tounge
  4. Roll around on the floor and jump on tables, taking special care to knock plates and cups onto the floor
    5. The cat will now welcome you as one of its own, you are a cat person now, yay!
